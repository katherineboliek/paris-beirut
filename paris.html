Two Days, Two Attacks

Paris

The Paris attack on November 13th, 2015 involved several coordinated events on Friday evening, November 13th. FIrst there were suicide bombers outside the Stade de France, then shootings and suicide bombings at restaurants. Lastly there was a mass shooting in the Bataclan where hostages were then held. 130 people were killed and there were 368 injuries.

Media

Articles were quickly written about the Paris attack before it was even over, including updates as it unfolded and analysis of the whole process afterwards. According to the Huffington Post, during the first 30 hours after the attack 4,507 media sources covered Paris.

Public

Hashtags PrayforParis, ParisAttacks and Paris shot up across Twitter and Instagram. Facebook revealed new features like the ability to check in as safe and temporary profile pictures overlaid with the French flag to show support.
