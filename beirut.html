Two Days, Two Attacks

Beirut

In Beirut, there were two suicide bombings in the marketplace of Bourj el-Barajneh, only a few minutes apart and several meters apart. A third man was discovered with explosives on him, but he was killed, most likely by one of the other blasts, before he could detonate his vest. 43 were killed in the attack and more than 200 were injured.

Public

The hashtags PrayforBeirut, BeirutAttacks and Beirut spiked at the same time as the Paris hashtags. Most people did not even know about the Beirut bombing until seeing it compared to Paris on their social media feeds. The trends for Beirut came about as people noticed that this similar tragedy had hardly been mentioned on their social media.
