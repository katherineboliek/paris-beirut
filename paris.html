Two Days, Two Attacks

Paris

The Paris attack involved several coordinated events on Friday evening, November 13th. FIrst there were suicide bombers outside the Stade de France, then shootings and suicide bombings at restaurants. Lastly there was a mass shooting in the Bataclan where hostages were then held. 130 people were killed and 368 injuries.
